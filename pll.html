Permutation of the last layer

Permutation of the Last Layer (PLL) solves the cube after the top face is completed.

Beginner PLL (2-look PLL) has 2 steps:

  1. Solve the corners (2 algorithms)
  2. Solve the edges (4 algorithms)

The corner algorithms are long, but very similar to each other. The edge algorithms are quite short and can be memorized visually.

Swap the corners:

Diagonal F R U' R' U' R U R' F' R U R' U' R' F R F'
Headlights R U R' U' R' F R2 U' R' U' R U R' F'

Swap the edges:

PLL (H) M2 U M2 U2 M2 U M2
PLL (Ua) R U' R U R U R U' R' U' R2
PLL (Ub) R2 U R U R' U' R' U' R' U R'
PLL (Z) M' U M2 U M2 U M' U2 M2
